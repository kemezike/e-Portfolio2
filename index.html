The Company

History

Wonder how coreDev Solutions, Incorporated initiated its name in the Accounting and IT world?

Twenty Five years ago, the very first version of our accounting software was developed and it was named as "iAccs" (Integrated Accounting System) in which it started as a DOS-based application. It has greatly helped Cooperatives and Rural Banks automate their Credit and Savings operation while enjoying integration into the Accounting System.

The rapid advancement in technology and the ever growing needs of our clients inspired us to further develop the system. In the year 2013, we launched the newest version of the system, we called it "iAccs 2013". We have evolved from being just a software provider into a service-oriented solutions provider. Thus the need to serve our clients and our aims to drastically broaden our horizons, brought the birth of coreDev, Solutions Incorported that serves its clients 24/7.

We offer every solution and provide the best service to our clients nationwide. We aim for customer satisfaction and innovation in developing safe, reliable and stable services to our clients, making every client as our only priority. CoreDev Solutions Incorporated is not just specialized in Accounting System, we also offer customized software development, hardware, network infrastructure deployment, and technical consultancy.

Organizational Structure

Department Assignment

Description and Scope of Responsibility

The department we trainees are assigned is the Research and Development Department. We are given projects to complete during our training period in the company. The project assigned to me is the Itinerary Management System, which can be used by the company to make the itinerary process more easy and simple.

Summary of Weekly Activities

Review and Research

The first phase of the project was to learn and review programming languages and frameworks, such as PHP and Laravel. By doing so, the development of the web system went smoothly. The company's programmers also guided us trainees to what to learn by teaching us personally and providing us learning materials, such as video tutorials and references.

I was able to learn more about web development and applied them during my training period.

Web Development

A Web Developer is responsible for designing, coding and modifying websites, from layout to function and according to a client's specifications. Strive to create visually appealing sites that feature user-friendly design and clear navigation.

Most weeks after the review and research went to Web Development, where we programmed the modules based on the companies requirements for the system. By using frameworks, the speed of development of the system, from the website UI to the functions and methods, was rapid.
